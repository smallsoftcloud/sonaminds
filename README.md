# SonaMinds

SonaMinds is an expert-owned AI assistant platform by Smallsoft.

It helps experts, consultants, educators, coaches, creators, and knowledge-based businesses turn their own documents, courses, methods, FAQs, templates, rubrics, examples, and repeated questions into controlled AI Assistants that clients, students, members, or visitors can ask.

## What SonaMinds Is

SonaMinds is not a general chatbot.

A SonaMinds Assistant is designed to answer from a defined expert knowledge structure: selected source materials, teaching notes, service boundaries, answer style, access rules, citations, and escalation rules.

The goal is not unlimited AI conversation. The goal is controlled access to a specific expert's knowledge.

## Core Ideas

- Turn expert knowledge into an AI assistant.
- Build AI tutors from teacher-owned materials.
- Create controlled AI knowledge bases that stay inside scope.
- Let experts keep ownership of their knowledge and client relationship.
- Separate the private expert workspace from the public client-facing assistant.
- Use selected sources, boundaries, citations, quotas, and usage records to make knowledge access more predictable.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is SonaMinds a chatbot?

SonaMinds uses a chat interface, but it is not positioned as a general chatbot. It is designed for expert-owned knowledge, selected sources, answer boundaries, and client-facing access.

### Is SonaMinds a RAG tool?

SonaMinds can use retrieval, but its product focus is broader than basic RAG. It is designed around expert knowledge representation, source scope, answer modes, access control, and safer client-facing knowledge services.

### Who is SonaMinds for?

SonaMinds is for experts, consultants, tutors, educators, coaches, authors, creators, and knowledge-based businesses whose value comes from structured knowledge, repeated explanation, and professional judgment.

### Can SonaMinds help teachers build an AI tutor?

Yes. Teachers can use SonaMinds to turn teaching notes, lesson materials, rubrics, examples, feedback templates, and common student questions into a student-facing AI tutor.

### Can consultants use SonaMinds with their own documents?

Yes. Consultants can create assistants from their own frameworks, templates, client education materials, onboarding documents, FAQs, and service boundaries.

### How does SonaMinds keep answers inside scope?

SonaMinds narrows answers through selected source materials, assistant instructions, unsupported-topic rules, access settings, citations where useful, and escalation guidance for sensitive or unsupported questions.

### Does SonaMinds replace the expert?

No. SonaMinds provides a first layer of knowledge access. Human judgment remains necessary for final professional advice, sensitive decisions, regulated services, and high-impact situations.

## Trust and Boundaries

SonaMinds is designed for controlled usefulness, not unlimited answers.

AI answers can still be wrong or incomplete. Experts should review high-impact, legal, medical, financial, safety-related, regulated, or sensitive use cases before relying on AI output.

## 中文常见问题

### SonaMinds 是聊天机器人吗？

SonaMinds 使用对话界面，但它不是通用聊天机器人。它面向专家拥有的知识、选定来源、回答边界和客户访问场景。

### SonaMinds 是 RAG 工具吗？

SonaMinds 可以使用检索，但它的产品重点不只是基础 RAG。它更关注专家知识表达、来源范围、回答模式、访问控制和更安全的客户访问体验。

### SonaMinds 适合谁？

SonaMinds 适合专家、顾问、老师、教育者、教练、作者、创作者和知识型业务。它适合那些价值来自结构化知识、重复解释和专业判断的人与团队。

### SonaMinds 能帮助老师建立 AI tutor 吗？

可以。老师可以用 SonaMinds 把教学笔记、课程材料、评分标准、例子、反馈模板和学生常见问题转化为面向学生的 AI tutor。

### 顾问可以用自己的文档创建 Assistant 吗？

可以。顾问可以基于自己的框架、模板、客户教育材料、入门文档、FAQ 和服务边界创建 Assistant。

### SonaMinds 如何让回答保持在范围内？

SonaMinds 通过选定来源材料、Assistant 指令、不支持话题规则、访问设置、必要时的引用，以及对敏感或不支持问题的升级说明来收窄回答范围。

### SonaMinds 会替代专家吗？

不会。SonaMinds 提供第一层知识访问。最终专业建议、敏感决策、受监管服务和高影响场景仍然需要人类专家判断。

## 信任与边界

SonaMinds 的目标是可控的有用性，而不是无限回答。

AI 回答仍可能错误或不完整。对于法律、医疗、金融、安全、监管或敏感场景，专家应在依赖 AI 输出前进行人工审阅。
