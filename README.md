### What Makes SonaMinds Different

SonaMinds is not a general chatbot, not a generic RAG demo, and not only a customer support widget. It is designed for expert-owned knowledge, controlled access, source-grounded answers, and client-facing assistant experiences.

### What SonaMinds Does

- Turns expert-owned knowledge into a client-facing AI Assistant.
- Answers from selected sources rather than broad, unbounded model knowledge.
- Shows source references where useful, so answers can be reviewed before people rely on them.
- Lets experts define the Assistant's name, introduction, tone, audience, supported topics, unsupported topics, and escalation rules.
- Separates the private expert workspace from the client access experience.
- Supports controlled access through links, access passes, quotas, expiry dates, seats, and usage records.
- Helps reduce repeated communication while keeping high-value judgment with the human expert.

### Who It Is For

SonaMinds is built for people and businesses whose value comes from structured knowledge, repeated explanation, and expert judgment.

- Experts and consultants can turn methods, templates, client materials, onboarding information, and repeated questions into a first layer of client support.
- Educators and creators can let students, readers, subscribers, or community members ask questions against courses, articles, transcripts, and knowledge archives.
- Small businesses can create lightweight knowledge support for product FAQs, onboarding, sales questions, help documents, and service explanations.

### How It Works

1. The expert creates a private workspace.
2. The expert uploads focused materials such as course notes, FAQs, templates, transcripts, policies, or method explanations.
3. The expert configures the Assistant's scope, tone, audience, supported topics, unsupported topics, and escalation behavior.
4. Clients, students, members, or visitors access the Assistant through controlled links or entry points.
5. The Assistant answers within the selected knowledge boundary and can include citations where useful.
6. The expert reviews usage, manages sources, adjusts access, and keeps the knowledge base current.

### Typical Use Cases

- Consulting preparation before a human session.
- Course support and learning material navigation.
- Creator archive access for articles, newsletters, podcasts, or evergreen resources.
- Membership knowledge support.
- Client onboarding and service explanation.
- Product FAQ and low-risk customer education.

### Trust and Boundaries

SonaMinds is designed for controlled usefulness, not unlimited answers. It narrows the answer space, grounds responses in expert-provided sources, and helps experts define when the Assistant should answer, refuse, redirect, or escalate.

SonaMinds does not replace the expert. Human judgment remains necessary for final professional advice, sensitive decisions, regulated services, and high-impact situations such as legal, medical, financial, safety, or accountability-related matters.

### SonaMinds 有何不同

SonaMinds 不是通用聊天机器人，不是普通 RAG 演示，也不只是客服插件。它面向专家拥有的知识、可控访问、基于来源的回答，以及面向客户的 Assistant 体验而设计。

### SonaMinds 能做什么

- 把专家拥有的知识转化为面向客户的 AI Assistant。
- 从专家选定的知识来源中回答，而不是依赖宽泛、无边界的通用模型知识。
- 在有帮助时显示来源引用，让回答在被依赖前可以被复查。
- 让专家设置 Assistant 的名称、介绍、语气、受众、支持话题、不支持话题和升级规则。
- 将私有专家工作区与客户访问体验分开。
- 通过链接、访问通行证、配额、过期时间、席位和使用记录控制访问。
- 减少重复沟通，同时把高价值判断保留给人类专家。

### 适合谁

SonaMinds 面向价值来自结构化知识、重复解释和专业判断的人与业务。

- 专家和顾问可以把方法论、模板、客户材料、入门信息和重复问题转化为客户支持的第一层入口。
- 教育者和创作者可以让学生、读者、订阅者或社群成员直接向课程、文章、转录稿和知识档案提问。
- 小型业务可以为产品 FAQ、入门指引、销售问题、帮助文档和服务说明创建轻量知识支持。

### 如何使用

1. 专家创建私有工作区。
2. 专家上传聚焦材料，例如课程笔记、FAQ、模板、转录稿、政策或方法说明。
3. 专家配置 Assistant 的范围、语气、受众、支持话题、不支持话题和升级方式。
4. 客户、学生、会员或访客通过受控链接或入口访问 Assistant。
5. Assistant 在选定知识边界内回答，并可在有帮助时提供引用。
6. 专家查看使用情况、管理知识来源、调整访问权限，并持续更新知识库。

### 典型场景

- 人工咨询前的客户准备。

- 创作者文章、通讯、播客或常青资源的档案访问。
- 会员知识支持。

- 产品 FAQ 和低风险客户教育。

### 信任与边界

SonaMinds 的目标是可控的有用性，而不是无限回答。它通过缩小回答范围、基于专家提供的来源回答，并帮助专家定义 Assistant 何时回答、拒绝、重定向或升级给人来降低风险。

SonaMinds 不替代专家。最终专业建议、敏感决策、受监管服务，以及法律、医疗、财务、安全或需要问责的高影响场景，仍需要人类专家负责判断和处理。
